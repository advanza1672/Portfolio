SWAAG Conference 2019

October 11, 2019

This past week, I went to the SWAAG meeting in Ft. Worth, TX where I competed in the undergraduate poster competition andthe Geobowl. I earned third place for my poster and my trivia team won second, though I can't claim much credit for that. I met some amazing people and enjoyed the opportunity to network and talk about my research interests with a group of like-minded people.
